Join the Team

We're always looking for talented people to help make Challenger Trials even better. If you've got the skills and the passion, pick a role below and show us what you can do.

Developer

Help us build and maintain the custom plugins and systems that make our events possible! We're looking for people who know their way around Java and the Spigot/Paper API.

Apply Now

Builder

Our events need amazing maps. If you've got an eye for detail and can create environments that look great and play even better, we want to see your portfolio.

Apply Now

Playtester

Before a game goes live, it needs to be broken. Join our playtesting team to help us balance mechanics, find bugs, and ensure every game is as fun as possible.

Apply Now

Photographer

Apply to be a photographer for the Challenger Trials staff team, you will take photos during the event to be posted on the official CT x and bsky pages.
